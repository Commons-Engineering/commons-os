A Network of Sovereign Commons

Each commons is independent, but it does not stand alone. The network makes every individual commons exponentially stronger.

SOVEREIGN BY DEFAULT
You own your data, your governance, and your infrastructure. There is no central authority dictating your operations.
SHARED PATTERNS
When one commons solves a problem, the solution becomes a pattern available to the entire network. Wisdom is shared globally, applied locally.
PULL, NOT PUSH
Updates and new patterns are never forced upon you. You pull what you need, when you need it, maintaining absolute control over your system.
