APPENDIX

The Five Qualities of a Commons

A canonical reference for the five non-negotiable qualities that define a modern commons.

Quality
Meaning
Test Question
Systematically
Value is produced by the system design and patterns, not by individuals.
Repeatable without exceptional individuals?
Alive
Regenerates through use: adapts, grows, or declines with attention.
Does this make the system more resilient?
Shared
Belongs to participants collectively; value is distributed among creators.
Serves those who enable it?
Just
Fairness across participation, purpose, and production; prevents enclosure.
Does architecture serve everyone fairly?
Built to last
Designed for longevity: rotating stewardship and documented knowledge.
Would it survive and thrive without us?
