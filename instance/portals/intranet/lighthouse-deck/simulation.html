Your Organisation, Years from Now

The Game fork is a simulation laboratory. It subjects your organisation to dozens of future scenarios simultaneously.

RUNNING YEARS AHEAD
While the Domain fork operates in the present, the Game fork is configured to run years into the future, testing the limits of your current architecture.
MULTIPLE DIMENSIONS
Simulating regulatory shifts, demographic changes, technology disruptions, and funding crises. Forcing the system to adapt, survive, and thrive.
THE CONSTRUCTION MANUAL
The Game and the Domain are two halves of one feedback loop. What breaks in simulation becomes a pattern that strengthens the real operating system.
