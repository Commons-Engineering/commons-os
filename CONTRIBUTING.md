# Contributing to Commons OS

Commons OS is a living framework. Contributions from forks strengthen the commons for everyone.

## How to contribute back

If you've created something in your fork that would benefit all Commons OS instances, you can contribute it upstream:

1. **Create a branch** in your fork for the contribution
2. **Open a Pull Request** against `commons-engineering/commons-os`
3. **Describe what it is** and why it benefits the commons
4. The Commons Engineering team will review and merge

## What we welcome

| Contribution | Where it goes |
|---|---|
| Improvements to specs | `_specifications/` |
| New core patterns | `_patterns/_core/` |
| Manifest updates | `_manifests/` |
| Template improvements | `_templates/` |
| Workflow improvements | `.github/workflows/` |
| Documentation | `README.md`, inline docs |

## What stays in your fork

Your `_local/` folder, your `CLAUDE.md`, and your instance-specific content are yours. Don't submit those upstream — they're your sovereign space.

## Conventions

- **Tables over lists** in markdown
- **Pattern format** must follow `_specifications/PATTERN_SPEC.md`
- **Lighthouse format** must follow the relevant lighthouse spec
- One idea per PR. Keep it focused.

## Questions?

Open an issue at [commons-engineering/commons-os](https://github.com/Commons-Engineering/commons-os/issues).
