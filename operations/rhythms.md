# Operating Rhythms

> Populated during boot and refined during operation.

| Rhythm | Cadence | What happens | Who |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Daily check-in** | Daily | Agent reviews board, proposes priorities | Agent + Founder |
| **Weekly review** | Weekly | Review progress, adjust priorities, close issues | Founder + Agent |
| **Monthly retrospective** | Monthly | Assess alignment, run ALIGN.md check | Founder + Agent |

---

*Adapt these rhythms to your commons. The improvement loop (GitHub Action) supplements but does not replace human rhythm.*
