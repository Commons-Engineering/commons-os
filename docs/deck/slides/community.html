What Your Commons Needs

The OS provides the infrastructure. You provide what makes it alive. A commons without domain expertise is an empty shell.

DOMAIN KNOWLEDGE
The deep, lived experience of your field. The nuances, the regulatory landscape, the specific challenges your domain faces. This is what fills the blueprint.
A COMMUNITY
The people who make up your ecosystem — patients, citizens, members, makers. The stakeholders who will participate in and benefit from the commons.
A PURPOSE
The driving force behind your system. The value it creates, the definition of justice it upholds, and the reason it needs to outlast its founders.
