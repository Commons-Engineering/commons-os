03 // GETTING STARTED

Three Steps to Your Commons

Everything you need is in the repository. Fork it, make it yours, and start building.

01
Fork the Repository
Clone the commons-os repo. It contains the full operating system: language, patterns, blueprints, and simulation tools. It's yours — CC-BY-SA licensed.
OUTPUT: YOUR_SOVEREIGN_REPO
02
Map Your Domain
Fill in the Domain fork with your reality: stakeholders, value streams, capabilities, and governance. The blueprint templates guide you through it.
OUTPUT: DOMAIN_BLUEPRINT
03
Simulate & Iterate
Create a Game fork. Inject future scenarios. Find where your model breaks and adapt. Repeat until your commons is resilient.
OUTPUT: RESILIENT_COMMONS

NEED GUIDANCE? JOIN THE COMMUNITY AT COMMONS.ENGINEERING OR FOLLOW THE JOURNEY ON SUBSTACK
